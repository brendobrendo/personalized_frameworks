### 🏛️ Structure Options
- **Donor-Advised Fund (DAF):**
  - Simple, low cost  
  - Less control  
- **Private Foundation:**
  - Full control over investments and grants  
  - Higher cost, more administration  
- **Endowment Model:**
  - Invest principal  
  - Distribute ~4–5% annually  
  - Preserve capital indefinitely  

---

### ⚖️ Tradeoffs (Be Transparent)
- Administrative complexity (especially foundations)  
- Required annual distributions (for foundations)  
- Ongoing oversight and governance  
- Less personal liquidity once assets are donated
